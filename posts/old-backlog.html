Old Backlog

This page details the different types of posts used in the Old Blog.
Before the switch, you were able to select a year to visit at the bottom.

GREEN POSTS

Usually saved for more casual and light-hearted topics.
These are the most common type of post, and are seen frequently throughout the blog.
NOTE: Posts labeled as "Green?" are usually more red in tone.

RED POSTS

Whenever I get upset or angry about something, I'll rant about it in these types of posts.
These, more aggresive and rant-y posts, will only come once in a long while.

BLUE POSTS

These are an uncommon type of post, specifically for when I feel especially sad and lonely.
I usually vent and go on about my struggles in these posts, and it can get personal.

MIDWAY POSTS

The least common type of blog post, also being much newer than the other colors.
These are reserved for topics that I feel are important and imminent enough to warrant their own blog post.
