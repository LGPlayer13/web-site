ABOUT THE BLOG

Welcome to the official Blog, where I ramble about stuff that's going on in my life.
New posts are written and uploaded the first week (as in the first 7 days) of every month.
Posts are sorted from newest (top) to oldest (bottom).

Dates are formatted YYYY-MM-DD. I don't know if I can change that...

POST TYPES

BLOG
Personal talking/ranting.
Blog posts are written by me every month, relaying a bunch of events that occur in my life.
They can also cover more personal issues that I may want to talk about.
DEVLOG
Project announcements/teasers.
Devlogs are written when a project of mine has a substantial release or update.
They can cover games, videos, music, or web site updates.
