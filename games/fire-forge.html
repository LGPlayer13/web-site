Released --/--/24 • Updated --/--/-- • v1.0 (alpha) // Tech-based

• IT STARTS AS A SIMPLE JOKE •
- Burning a scrap during the 1st quest.
-----
Fire Forge is an upcoming tech-based game where you burn random junk in an open fire, climbing the odd tech tree along the way. Make weird materials, build contraptions, and chat with your wacky friend.

Starting off as a simple joke, this game's plot spirals into a chaotic mess of multi-management, growing a friendship, and researching literally everything you can to... have something to do during summer break.

• HOW'D THIS HAPPEN •
Having your Friend forage for items while burning a scrap. -
-----
Once The Lost Labyrinths a1.2.1 was released, I started development on a prototype known as Embers. Its plot was wildly different from that of Fire Forge's, but it laid the groundwork for its mechanics.

After establishing FF's plot and layout, I began work on it at the beginning of 2024, and it's been in the oven ever since. It'll be out (hopefully) by late November.

• WHAT IT HAS TO OFFER •
- Cooking some "delicious" "treats" while your friend forages.
