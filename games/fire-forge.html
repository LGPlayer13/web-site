Fire Forge is a (shelved) tech-based game where you burn random junk in an open fire, climbing the odd tech tree along the way.
Make weird materials, build contraptions, and chat with your wacky friend.

Starting off as a simple joke, this game's plot spirals into a chaotic mess of multi-management and growing a friendship, all to have something to do during summer break.

Once The Lost Labyrinths a1.2.1 was released, I started development on a prototype known as Embers.
Its plot was wildly different from that of Fire Forge's, but it laid the groundwork for its mechanics.

After establishing FF's plot and layout, I began work on it at the beginning of 2024, and it's been in the oven ever since.

Not sure if it'll ever be released, but I may come back to it later.
