THE CHAPTER IN-BETWEEN

Lethal Labyrinth, level 1 of the game.
George 1.5 is an upcoming platformer game that is the chapter between the first and the next. Explore the most unknown parts of the archipelago as George goes on a physical and mental journey he'll never forget.

After over 3 years of development hell and constantly switching engines, the next installment in the George series is finally (and properly) being worked on, taking place right after where the first left off.

HOW'D THIS HAPPEN

George and Greg beneath the Labyrinth, at a terminal.
Soon after the original George 1 was released, work on George 2 began. Inspired by a multitude of other Scratch platformers, it was slated to release mid-late 2022. However, those plans eventually fell through.

After going through four engines in total, I decided to scrap George 2 and start a new "in-between" chapter of the series. Its story is much more fleshed out and well-written than its predecessor's, and the gameplay is much more smooth and stable.
