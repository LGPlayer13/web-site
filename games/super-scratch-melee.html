Super Scratch Melee is a turn-based fighting game where you duel in (somewhat) action-packed battles.
It was made back in 2021, and is an incredibly outdated game. Still recommend checking it out.

Boasting a whopping 14 fighters and several unique modes, this game has you fight against random characters, challenge different bosses, and customize a fighter of your own!

Being inspired by classic Scratch games like Super Scratch Smash and Super Scratch Brawl (may it rest in piece), I felt the need to continue this fighting game trend.

Since I wasn't qualified to make a platforming-fighting game, I decided to go for a turn-based approach, while still keeping the "Super Scratch" title.
It wasn't very good, but I still look back on it fondly.

It also features...

  • 14 fighters with unique movesets and abilities
  • 18+ different stages to fight on
  • 4 bosses to duke out against in Classic Mode
  • A multitude of extras, including a Boss Rush and Shop for unlockables
  • A bonus customizable fighter (PLC)
  • Stock and Timed battle types
  • Four difficulties to choose from in Classic Mode
