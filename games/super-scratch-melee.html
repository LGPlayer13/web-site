• A CLASSIC ACTION-PACKED PROJECT •

-----
Super Scratch Melee is a turn-based fighting game where you choose to play as a fighter and duel in (somewhat) action-packed battles. Duke it out with a friend or a CPU in this action-packed, turn-based game, with over 10 fighters!

Boasting a whopping 14 fighters and several unique modes, this game has you fight against random characters, challenge different bosses, and customize a fighter of your own!

• HOW'D THIS HAPPEN •
Ravioli using a Support while weakened in battle. -
-----
After being inspired by classic Scratch games like Super Scratch Smash and Super Scratch Brawl (may its unfinished soul rest in piece), I felt the need to continue this fighting game trend.

Since I wasn't qualified to make a platforming-fighting game, I decided to go for a turn-based approach, while still keeping the "Super Scratch" title. It wasn't very good, but I look back on it fondly.
