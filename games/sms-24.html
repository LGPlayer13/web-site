OUT WITH THE OLD, IN WITH THE NEW

The beginning of the game, up to the 3rd building.
Stone Mine Simulator 2024 (aka SMS '24) is a clicker game where mine stone, buy strange buildings, and play minigames to mine more stone.

As you start off with nothing but a pickaxe and hopes and dreams, you slowly make your way up the tech tree to research different upgrades and oddities to achieve ultimate production.

HOW'D THIS HAPPEN

A bit after the beginning, up to the 6th building.
Before the school year started, I wanted to make one last project before my life became flooded with homework and chores. I thought making a neat clicker game would be great for this.

The original Stone Mine Simulator games were greatly lacking in quality and decent art, so this release aimed to revisit what them, properly fleshing them out with balanced mechanics and improved artwork.

WHAT IT HAS TO OFFER

The midpoint of the game, right before research.
  • 7 base buildings, with 3 more unlockable through research
  • Over 80 upgrades to buy, all with amusing flavor texts
  • Over 100 achievements to unlock, plus 3 secret ones
  • Research system with 5 different trees of unique upgrades
  • Spellbook minigame for casting spells
  • Bakery minigame to provide passive buffs
