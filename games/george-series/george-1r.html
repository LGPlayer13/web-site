George 1 Remastered is a reimagining of the very first entry in the George Series. In celebration of my Scratch account's 3-year-anniversary, this project was made as a commemoration of my time there.

Taking what little the original project had, this game completely overhauls it with polished graphics, slightly more engaging gameplay, and new features that breathe life into this simple platforming game.
Inspired by classic Scratch platformers such as Chirpy the Penguin and Speedy, the original version of George 1 aimed to expand this little character I made for an animation nobody's ever seen.

It introduces staple characters like Greg and Cory to expand the universe, making it stand out and become something pretty special. A sequel is planned, but is currently going through... issues.
It also features...
  • Five base levels that offer challenging gameplay
  • Animated cutscenes, providing context for whatever the hell is happening
  • The Museum, filled with unseen content
  • Speedrun Mode, which allows players to race for the record time
  • Endless Mode, which brings infinite fun for the whole family
  • Bonus Level 6: the ultimate challenge!!
  • Cool widescreen port hosted on itch.io
