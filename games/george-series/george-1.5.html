George 1.5 is an upcoming platformer game that is the chapter between the first and the next. Explore the most unknown parts of the archipelago as George goes on a physical and mental journey he'll never forget.

After over 3 and a half years of development hell and constantly switching engines, the next installment in the George Series is in a stable engine, taking place right after where the first left off.
Months after the original George 1 was released, work on George 2 began. Inspired by a multitude of other Scratch platformers, it was slated to release mid-late 2022. However, those plans eventually fell through.

After going through four engines in total, I decided to scrap George 2 and start a new "in-between" chapter of the series. Its story is much more fleshed out and well-written than its predecessor's, and the gameplay is much more smooth and stable.
It also features...
  • Five base levels packed with unique mechanics, hazards, and bosses
  • Plentiful cutscenes throughout the game, both animated and in-game
  • Bloated asset count to ensure memory leaks
  • Multiple post-launch updates planned
  • Original soundtrack by hyphen-lost!
  • Pausing, saving, ranks, timeline, and more!
