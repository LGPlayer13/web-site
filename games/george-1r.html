• THE FIRST ENTRY IN THE SERIES •
- Rocky Roughs, level 1 of the game.
-----
George 1 Remastered is a reimagining of the very first entry in the George Series. In celebration of my Scratch account's 3-year-anniversary, this project was made as a commemoration of my time there.

Taking what little the original project had, this game completely overhauls George the Jumping Triangle with new, polished graphics, slightly more engaging gameplay, and new features that breathe life into this dinky platforming game.
• HOW'D THIS HAPPEN •
Cerulean City, level 3 of the game. -
-----
Inspired by classic Scratch platformers such as Chirpy the Penguin and Speedy, the original version of George 1 aimed to expand this little character I made for an animation nobody's ever seen.

It introduces new, classic characters like Greg and Cory to expand the universe, making it stand out and become something pretty special.
