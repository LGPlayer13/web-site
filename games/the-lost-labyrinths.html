• INTO A NEW ADVENTURE •
- The results of fighting a slime.
-----
The Lost Labyrinths is a real-time fighting game where you fight different monsters, utilize different systems and mechanics to craft unique gear, and use every advantage you have to reach the Labyrinths.

Being the second IGM game I made after my return in 2022, this was the start of a brand new legacy that is still growing to this very day.
• HOW'D THIS HAPPEN •
A casual mining trip in the Coal Mines. -
-----
After abandoning Stone Mine Simulator 2 for something fresh and original, I was motivated by Forez's Dream to create an RPG-like game of my own.

Development of the game was started back in July 2022, and it was launched almost a month later to positive reception. It's gone on hiatus since, but I have not completely abandoned the project.
• WHAT IT HAS TO OFFER •
- The results of a zombie battle with a bone knife.
-----
●ㅤ6 unique monsters to fight, with their own strengths and abilities
●ㅤOver 50 tools and armor to collect and use
●ㅤ5-ish different spells to use for an advantage during battles
●ㅤFishing system for catching fish to use in potion-making
●ㅤMining system to use for forging new tools and armor to use
●ㅤDeal system for snagging some extra cash
