Sid's Adventure is a side-scrolling game where you play as Sid, who wakes up to find himself in a colorless void. After the entire world was devastated by darkness, it's up to him to save it!

Featuring over 7 quirky characters and 15 tasks to complete, this game has you talk to people, do fun little quests, and explore around the area. It's pretty wacky.
Starting off as a shelved project back from mid-2022, I decided to revive this project after being burnt out from the development hell that was George 2.

Inspired by nothing in particular, the game features a unique cast of characters that are all based off real-life shapes! (except for Robert, who doesn't really fit but he's still there anyway)
It also features...
  • 1 chapter so far, with more to come later (maybe)
  • 8 characters, all with different personalities and witty dialogue
  • 17 total tasks, most of which have you doing questionable chores
  • Semi-reliable saving and loading system
  • An actual, kinda well-put-together trailer
