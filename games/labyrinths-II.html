Labyrinths II is a fast-paced, text-based dungeon crawler. It's the sequel to The Lost Labyrinths, which was originally made back in late 2022.
Explore through the many different labyrinths, collect loot to craft better gear, and face off against the Brutes.

The first alpha contains 3 labyrinths to explore, tons of loot to collect, and art made by hyphen-lost!
After being inspired by games like Forez's Dream 2 and Waves, I started to make a prototype for Labyrinths II. It was nothing more than a test labyrinth and some basic gear. This was the pre-alpha version.

Soon enough, I developed more interesting mechanics and got a buddy of mine to make the game's art. A month of semi-active development later, the first alpha has officially released.
It also features...
  • 3 labyrinths to explore (so far!)
  • Dozens of tools and armor pieces to collect and increase your stats
  • Fishing and mining mechanics for obtaining resources
  • Deal system for bonus XP and resources
  • 2 spells and 4 potions at your disposal
  • Sleek, yet immersive layout/CSS
  • ...more to come soon!
